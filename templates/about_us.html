Who Are We?

We Connect is a National University of Sicence and Technology final year project that aims to offer an Arabic sign language web application for deaf and mute people.

Who Are We Targeting?

We Connect's main target is the Arab community as other communities already have several projects available, whereas the Arab community has a lack of this type of projects and solutions.

Why Choose a Web App as a Platform?

We Connect chose a web application as a platform because it will serve more devices and platforms. Rather than creating a mobile application that will only serve mobile phones, we decided to serve all devices that have a camera and internet. In addition, a web application will reduce the system requirements significantly compared to if it were a mobile application, as most of the operations will happen on the web rather than on the device.
